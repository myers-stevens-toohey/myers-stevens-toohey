About Us

Founded in 1970, Myers-Stevens & Toohey & Co., Inc. provides its clients with superior customer service, state of-the-art technology and over 45 years of experience and knowledge in the very specialized area of student accident and sickness insurance.

As a fully licensed Managing General Agency/Third-Party Administrator (MGA/TPA), we provide carefully integrated insurance solutions to thousands of school systems throughout the Western and Midwestern states. Our clients include school districts, private and charter schools, colleges, consortiums, JPAs, amateur/youth sports programs, foundations, booster clubs and similar organizations serving the needs of young people.

A strong customer focus has always been the benchmark of Myers-Stevens & Toohey & Co., Inc. This commitment has enabled the firm to deliver superior professional services to its clients exceeding industry standards. These services include claims adjudication, loss analysis and claims reporting, policy issuance, product design, printing and fulfillment.

Myers-Stevens & Toohey & Co., Inc.'s headquarters are located in Southern California in the city of Mission Viejo. Key elements, including product design, administrative services, and marketing are performed at this facility.

For more information about Myers-Stevens & Toohey & Co., Inc., please email us at info@myers-stevens.com or contact us toll free at (800) 827-4695.
