Accidental Death & Dismemberment

Why you need AD&D insurance coverage?

Due to the inherent risks placed upon firefighters accidental death and dismemberment is a real concern. Enhance your current life insurance coverage with Accidental Death and Dismemberment coverage for minimal cost. It is an excellent and inexpensive way to protect against the financial devastation a catastrophic accident can have on your family.

COST:

The premium rate for you and your spouse is $1.25 per person each month for every $25,000 of AD&D insurance coverage.
