SAFER Grant

CSFEWBC's SAFER GRANT has provided benefits at "No Cost" to over 230 departments, representing over 6,000 Volunteer Emergency Responders in California.

HAVE YOU ENROLLED? This is a No Cost Benefit for Your Department

The insurance branch of CSFA, California State Firefighters' Employee Welfare Benefit Corporation (CSFEWBC) has been fortunate to receive a $2.2 Million SAFER Grant. The SAFER Grant provides the following on-duty insurance benefits:

  • $500 per week in Disability Benefits, payable for up to 104 weeks
  • $100,000 Accidental Death and Dismemberment coverage
