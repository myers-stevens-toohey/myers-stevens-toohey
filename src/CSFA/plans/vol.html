Volunteer Coverage

Our Volunteers risk their lives and face serious injury every time they answer the call. The CA Workers' Compensation will provide benefits, however it will more than likely not cover all costs associated with the firefighters lost wages and expenses. These shortcomings can cripple a family's financial stability. That's where our Provident Accident & Health coverage can make a real difference by filling in the gaps and providing benefits that Workers' Compensation does not offer.
