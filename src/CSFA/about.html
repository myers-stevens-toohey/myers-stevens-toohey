About Us

Founded in 1970, Myers-Stevens & Toohey & Co., Inc. (MST) is a fully licensed broker/administrator providing insurance solutions to a wide variety of clients, including professional associations, municipalities, and government entities.

A strong customer focus has always been the benchmark of MST. This commitment has enabled the firm to deliver superior professional services to its clients exceeding industry standards. MST has been providing association-sponsored group insurance programs to actively working and retired members of the California State Firefighters Association (CSFA) since 1967. We pride ourselves on the longevity of this relationship and attribute it to being a service-oriented company specializing in association benefit programs.

MST's headquarters are located in Southern California in the city of Mission Viejo. The company maintains a regional office strategically located near the State's capital in Roseville, California. Sacramento is home to many of the professional associations we currently service. MST provides a turn-key solution for their clients, including product design, administrative services, marketing, printing, regional sales support, claims administration, and customer service.
