Accidental Death & Dismemberment

Considering the high risks associated with working as a Peace Officer, AD&D insurance coverage can provide an inexpensive level of protection for you and your family against the financial impact of a fatal accident. AD&D insurance coverage is an excellent way to supplement to your existing life insurance against the unexpected.

Disability Coverage

Simply put, this program protects your paycheck 24/7 in the event you're disabled and can't work. While your paycheck may be gone, your bills and your family's living expenses will continue. The Insurance and Benefits Trust of PORAC (I&B Trust) Disability program will enable you to continue to meet those financial obligations

Members are living a lot longer. That's the good news. But increased longevity has created a new financial dilemma: how to prepare for the high cost of old age.
