School Plans

Some of the many plans we offer are tailored specifically for JPAs, school districts, private schools, universities, colleges, and special niche groups. These plans are provided so that our clients can be certain that the proper coverage is in place when it is needed most. Available plans range from field trip coverage to catastrophic accident coverage.

Learn more about School Plans

Student Plans

Many of the plans we offer are available for students starting in pre-school and going through 12th grade with select offerings for college students. Our offerings range from student health & accident plans to prescription drug discounts and even interscholastic tackle football coverage. These plans are made available through our on going relationships with over 20,000 schools in the West.Various levels of coverage are available and eligibility for coverage is determined by the state the applicant resides in and whether or not the applicant’s JPA, school district, private school, university, or college has an existing relationship with Myers-Stevens & Toohey & Co. Inc..
